# Finding: Alignment Debt in African AI Users

## Source
Oyemike et al. (2025)
"Alignment Debt: Measuring the Hidden Cost of AI Misalignment for African Users"

## Key Finding
Among 411 AI users in Kenya and Nigeria:
- 33.8% faced epistemic misalignment in AI outputs
- Users experiencing epistemic challenges verified AI outputs at significantly higher rates
- African users have developed invisible compensatory strategies for displacement
