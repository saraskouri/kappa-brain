# Finding: AfriStereo — Stereotype Perpetuation in African AI Contexts

## Source
AfriStereo (2024)
"Stereotypes and Bias in AI Models Applied to African Contexts"

## Key Finding
- AI models trained on Global North datasets perpetuate stereotypes in African contexts
- AI-generated images depict African individuals in impoverished settings even when
  prompts explicitly challenge such narratives
- The bias operates at the output layer even when prompts resist it

## Relevance to CDI
This finding validates the two-layer distortion model in Section 3.3 of the paper.
The bias persists at the output layer regardless of prompt-level resistance —
exactly as the training layer / output layer distinction predicts.

## Connection to P-E-A Extension
AfriStereo documents the expressed distortion (Layer 2).
CDI measures both layers simultaneously through RAS, ATD, and LLS.
